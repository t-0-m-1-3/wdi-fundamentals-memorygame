Instructions

Concentration, also known as match Match, memory, Pelmanism, Shinkei-suijaku, Pexesco or simply Pairs, is a card game in which all of the cards are laid face down on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over the pairs of matching cards.


Memory games are great ways to enhance you ability to retain and build Nerualplasticity. It's like doing push-ups for your neurons.
